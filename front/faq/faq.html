Frequently Asked Questions

How to connect to my Whatsapp?

To connect to your whatsapp web you need to click on 'TRY WAB' button, then a whatsapp web window will open with QR code that you need to scan.

How to clean my history messages?

To clean your history messages you need to open the menu --> 'history' --> 'clear history'

How to relode whatsapp?

To reload whatsapp window click on 'Reload Whatsapp' button.

Can I enter any number?

No, this app works only with the chats names in your Whatsapp.

I have a problem with the app, what can I do?

If you encounter a problem with the app please contact us so we can help you.
